Cvent has unveiled the finalists for its 14th annual Excellence Awards program, recognizing organizations of every size that are reshaping meetings, events, and hospitality through innovative approaches and measurable outcomes. The awards ceremony will take place during Cvent CONNECT in Nashville on July 14, and this year introduces new categories including Digital Events Visionary, Event Intelligence Award, Association & Non-Profit Impact Icon, and The AI Activator.
